Welcome to DrawKit¶

Welcome to DrawKit. DrawKit is an illustration framework for Mac OS X. With DrawKit you can quickly add vector drawing and illustration features to your Mac applications.

DrawKit is comprehensive, powerful, and complete. The design is modular and can be extended to meet your application’s needs.

DrawKit is licensed under the BSD Licence and can be included in commercial applications.

Download the latest DrawKit source code from DrawKit/DrawKit over on github.
